<H1><strong>Privacy Policy – SafeLimit</strong></H1>

Last updated: July 2026

SafeLimit is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor selected LinkedIn activity. This Privacy Policy explains what information SafeLimit processes and how it is used.

<h2>Information SafeLimit processes</h2>

SafeLimit processes and stores the following information locally on your device:

- Counts of LinkedIn actions, including reactions, comments, connection requests, and profile visits
- Daily activity history and hourly activity totals
- Settings and preferences, such as dark mode
- Your own LinkedIn profile path, such as /in/username, to avoid counting visits to your own profile
- A Pro licence key, when entered
- Local date and time information used for daily resets and activity statistics

SafeLimit reads limited LinkedIn page information, including button labels, links, and URL paths, to detect relevant actions.

SafeLimit does not store a list of the specific third-party profiles, companies, or schools you visit.

<h2>Information SafeLimit does not access</h2>

SafeLimit DOES NOT:

- Access your LinkedIn password, credentials, or private messages
- Store the content of your posts, comments, or messages
- Collect your email address through the extension
- Use the LinkedIn API
- Sell your information
- Use your information for advertising or unrelated tracking
- Storage and data sharing

SafeLimit stores extension data locally using Chrome’s `chrome.storage.local` API.

The current version does not send activity data, LinkedIn page information, settings, or licence keys to SafeLimit’s developer or to third parties.

Because this information remains on your device, the developer cannot access or recover it.

<h2>Licence keys</h2>

When you enter a Pro licence key, SafeLimit checks its format and stores it locally.

The current version does not send licence keys to an external verification server.

<h2>External services</h2>

SafeLimit may open external pages, such as a Tally waitlist form or your email application.

SafeLimit does not automatically send locally stored extension data to these services. Information you voluntarily provide on an external website is governed by that service’s privacy policy.

<h2>Permissions</h2>

SafeLimit uses the following Chrome permissions:

- **Storage:** Saves activity counts, history, preferences, profile-path information, and licence data locally
- **Alarms:** Resets activity information and the extension badge at midnight
- **Tabs:** Checks whether the active tab is a LinkedIn page and opens LinkedIn when needed
- **Access to linkedin.com:** Displays SafeLimit and detects the LinkedIn actions described in this policy

SafeLimit uses information received from Chrome APIs only to provide its disclosed extension functionality and complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

<h2>Data retention and deletion</h2>

Information remains stored on your device until you:

- Clear or reset the data through SafeLimit
- Remove the extension
- Clear the extension’s storage through Chrome

<h2>Children’s privacy</h2>

SafeLimit is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>

We may update this Privacy Policy when SafeLimit’s features or data practices change. The updated version will be published with a revised date.
